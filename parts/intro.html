About SpinW

Spin Wave MATLAB Library

  • Spin wave simulation for commensurate / incommensurate magnetic structures
  • Classical Monte Carlo simulation and mean field optimisation of GS
  • spinw class and set of functions

For neutron scatterers

  • Easy to create spin models
  • From simple lattice (chain, triangular, kagome) to complete magnetic crystals
  • Include further neighbor interaction to existing models
  • Calculates magnetic diffuse scattering
